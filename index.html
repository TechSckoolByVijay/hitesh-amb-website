Welcome to Amit Mishtan Bhandar

For over 80 years, Amit Mishtan Bhandar has been serving the finest Laddu (लड्डू), Peda (पेड़ा), Swamini packs, and Prasad (प्रसाद) to the people of Chirawa (Chirawa) and surrounding areas including Khatu and Salasar. Our traditional mithai (मिठाई) are made with pure ingredients, authentic recipes, and devotion to quality.

Located in the heart of Chirawa, Jhunjhunu district, Rajasthan, we specialize in creating sweets that are perfect for religious offerings, festivals, celebrations, and daily enjoyment. Our Prasad items are especially popular among devotees visiting the sacred temples of Khatushyam Baba and Lord Hanuman at Salasar.

Best Laddu & Peda Shop in Chirawa — Our signature Laddu and Peda are crafted using time-honored techniques passed down through generations. Each sweet is made fresh daily with premium ghee, pure milk, and the finest ingredients to ensure authentic taste and quality.
