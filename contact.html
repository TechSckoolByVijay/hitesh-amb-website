Frequently Asked Questions

What are your most popular items?

Our signature Besan Laddu, Milk Peda, and Prasad Thalis are customer favorites. We also specialize in Swamini packs for religious ceremonies.

Do you deliver to Khatu and Salasar?

Yes! We deliver authentic Rajasthan sweets to Khatu, Salasar, and all towns in Jhunjhunu district. Special packaging available for temple Prasad.

Can I order for same-day delivery in Chirawa?

Yes, same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2 PM within Chirawa (Chirawa) and immediate surrounding areas.

Do you accept bulk orders for weddings?

Absolutely! We offer special pricing for bulk orders of Laddu, Peda, and Swamini packs. Please contact us 24-48 hours in advance.

Are your sweets made fresh daily?

Yes, all our Laddu, Peda, and Prasad items are made fresh every day using traditional recipes and pure ingredients in our Chirawa shop.
